Kelsey's Feeline Beeline


We’ve been in the St. Louis area since 2000. The first time I took my cat outside in a safe environment I knew I had to share this with all cat families.


Our focus is to make sure your cat gets the adventure of a lifetime. The first two sessions will focus on training your cat to be comfortable wearing the harness. Training will take patience and plenty of treats. Not all cats are meant for the harness life, if at the end of the second session it seems like your cat isn’t about adventure that session will be free.


The younger we train the better!! New kitties are always ready to learn, explore, and earn treats. Having a cat that is food orientated will also help! If you purchase our basic package you’ll get an hour of outdoor time, a half hour of playtime and a guarantee of fresh food and water. Of course your kitty will get a treat for behaving outside.


We want every cat to feel like the King/Queen of their yard!
