About Me


I’m 24 years old and all my life I have always had at least one cat in my household. I knew one day my life’s call would revolve around our feline friends. You won’t find anyone else that loves cats as much as I do. I currently have two wonderful cats. My senior citizen Sabrina will be turning 11 this year. She’s grey and white with vibrant green eyes. She’s always at home on my lap, purring. If it was her choice (and mine) we would lay in bed all day taking sun naps. And then there’s my little rascal, Percy. He’s a 16 pound tuxedo with yellow eyes and is only 3 years old. He’s the one who showed me that cats deserved to be walked outside. He’s always at home in his box and in the kitchen where we keep the cat food. He’s very food orientated so it was very easy to train him to wear a harness.
